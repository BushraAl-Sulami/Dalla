Al Balad

Al Balad Heritage District


Al-Balad is an old historical neighborhood in the city of Jeddah, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and is considered one of its most prominent cultural and heritage landmarks. Al balad is characterized by its narrow streets and traditional buildings built of coral stone and wood, which reflect the traditional architectural style of the Hejaz region. Al-Balad is famous for its traditional markets such as Souq Al-Alawi and Souq Al-Bedouin, where visitors can experience shopping in a historical atmosphere and purchase traditional products such as perfumes, incense, and spices. Al-Balad was included on the UNESCO World Heritage List in 2014 due to its historical and cultural importance. The country area is home to many notable landmarks.
